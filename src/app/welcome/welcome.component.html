Participation information:

Thank you for participating in our study. In this survey, we would like to assess your performance and experience while playing the game Tetris. This study consists of three parts. In each part, you have to play the Tetris game and fill the questionnaire related to your overall performance and experience. It will take about approximately 30 minutes to complete the study. The goal of this study is to determine your performance and experience while playing Tetris under different conditions. Your data will be stored and processed anonymously. More information on the data protection can be found in the Data Protection Notice. This study is being conducted by Sridhar Karra as part of his master thesis

Notes on data protection and participation information :

  I have read and understood the notes on Data Protection and the participation information and agree that my data will be used anonymously for scientific research purposes.

General information

The following study is part of a master thesis at the Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Künstliche Intelligenz (DFKI) and Universität des Saarlandes (UdS).

Approval from the Ethical Review Board

This study is approved by the Ethical Review Board of the department of Computer Science at Saarland University stating that there are no ethical concerns against the implementation of this research project.

Voluntary participation and consent

Participation in the study is voluntary. Consent to participate in this study can be revoked at any time and without giving reasons. You can close the browser window before submitting your questionnaire. All incomplete data records will be deleted by us. After the end of the data collection, the legal rights of revocation, information, correction, blocking and deletion can no longer be exercised, because the data can no longer be assigned to the data subject.

What data do we collect?

After consenting to the data collection, the following demographic data will be collected and processed anonymously:
  • Age group
  • Gender
  • Nationality
The following game-specific and questionnaire data are collected:
  • Game-related data
  • Questions based on prior Tetris experience
  • Questions based on overall performance/experience for each game condition
  • Questionnaire based on Player Experience Inventory (PXI)
  • Questionnaire based on Intrinsic Motivation Inventory (IMI)
All the data is stored locally on the web browser which is used to participate in this study. Once the data is submitted, it is then stored in the external server at https://account.mongodb.com/. We use Mongo DB as our external database server.

Anonymization of data

With the transmission of the collected data to our server, an anonymization takes place, which makes an allocation of the collected data to a person practically impossible, if no personal data are entered in free text fields.

What do we use the collected data for?

The results and data will be used exclusively for scientific research purposes and they will be published in anonymous form as part of the final thesis and as a scientific publication of this study. Furthermore, they can be further processed in anonymous form for other scientific research purposes.
