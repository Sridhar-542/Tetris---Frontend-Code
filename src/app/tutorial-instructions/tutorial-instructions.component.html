In the next step, you will play the game Tetris.

  • Your goal will be to align the falling blocks in a way, that that they fill horizontal lines. Each completed line will grant you points.

  • A manual with the controls will be shown to the left of the game.

  • In this first run, you can take your time to understand the game mechanics as the score will not be counted.

  • You can play the game until you see Game Over on the screen and then click Continue to start the actual study .

  • Please feel free to contact your supervisor, in case if you have any questions. Good luck!
